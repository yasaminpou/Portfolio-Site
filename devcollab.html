devCollab a space for Software engineers and Developers to build, expand, and improve business.

How it all started?

Through my work with Software engineers and developers I noticed a gap for a type of specialized freelancer. While other freelance platforms offer a variety of freelance services.

My role in this project

UX/UI designer

Duration of the project

From November 2020 to April 2021

Design Process

Design process for devCollab started by competitive analysis. It was very important to have in-depth knowledge regarding competitors and how devCollab can be different from others to stand out among targeted audiences. After this the wireframing, and prototyping for devCollab has started.

Design iteration

After the initial design, I made many iteration to the design based on rounds of user testing, making my design based on best practices for accessibility and also making sure to be updated with all new changes to iOS. Please take a look at the picture folder for all design pictures and some of the documentation.

Take away

devCollab has gone under many iterations, during this process I have learned how to make changes into my design based on user feedback, also making sure each change stays within the guidelines and not compromising the original idea. This has been an interesting, exciting, and challenging process, but for sure very rewarding.
